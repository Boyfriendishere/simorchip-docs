320 computational vectors initialized. Pipeline at steady frequency.
A proven 28nm process positions 320 high-efficiency stream processors at 780 MHz, communicating in perfect sync across the 128-Bit data highway. Dense financial visualization layers, heavy browser UI engines, multiple video feeds — the architecture commands the load with unyielding authority and minimal power draw.
128-Bit — Double the throughput of 64-Bit pipelines
The R5230 series runs a 64-Bit bus. The R7240 runs 128-Bit — twice the data highway width per clock. At the same GDDR3 frequency, the 128-Bit bus moves more data per cycle without requiring higher clocks or more power. The pipeline stays clear under sustained multi-display load.
PCIe 3.0 × 8 — Faster uplink than the R5230
PCIe 3.0 delivers approximately double the per-lane bandwidth of PCIe 2.0. On an × 8 slot, the R7240 has significantly more headroom for instruction throughput than its predecessor. The uplink never becomes a bottleneck for display-class workloads.
