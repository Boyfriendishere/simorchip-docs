The GTX 750 Ti has 640.

4GB GDDR5 — a different class of memory.
GDDR5 doesn't just hold more — it moves data faster.
1250 MHz. 128-bit bus. At full load, the GTX 750 Ti processes image data faster than GDDR3 cards can even receive it.

PCIe 3.0 — double the bandwidth.
PCIe 3.0 × 16 doubles the bandwidth available between the card and your system compared to PCIe 2.0.
The system talks to the card faster. The card responds faster. Everything moves faster.
