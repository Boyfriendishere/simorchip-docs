On-Die ECC · PMIC · ETT Verified · Lifetime Warranty

#DDR5 #4800 #U-DIMM #SO-DIMM #On-Die ECC #PMIC #Lifetime Warranty

DDR5 Exclusive Technologies

On-Die ECC

ECC built directly into each chip. Corrects bit errors automatically — no CPU involvement. Always on, zero overhead.

PMIC

Dedicated Power Management IC on the module. Stable 1.1V delivery — 8% more efficient than DDR4.

Dual 32-bit Channels

Two independent 32-bit channels replace DDR4's single 64-bit. More bank groups, higher parallelism.

WHY DDR5?

Bandwidth44.8 GB/sUp to 44.8 GB/s — 50% above DDR4-3200. Meaningful improvement across every workload.
Voltage1.1V8% lower than DDR4's 1.2V. PMIC on the module ensures stable, efficient power delivery.
Speed4800 Mbps4800 / 5600 Mbps supported. DDR5-5600 for maximum bandwidth; DDR5-4800 for broader platform compatibility.
On-Die ECCBuilt-inECC integrated into every chip. Always active, no CPU overhead. Corrects bit errors automatically — better data accuracy every read.
PMICOn-moduleDedicated Power Management IC on the DDR5 module. Stable 1.1V delivery — more efficient and precise than motherboard-side power management.
CompatibilityJEDECJEDEC and RoHS compliant. ETT-verified original factory chips with full high/low temperature environmental screening.
WarrantyLifetimeLifetime limited warranty across the entire Simorchip memory lineup. Buy once, covered for life.
