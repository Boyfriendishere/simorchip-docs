Behind the intelligence

  • SLC Caching — Intelligent SLC cache boosts burst read/write performance. File transfers, boots, app launches — all faster from day one.
  • Dynamic Thermal Throttling — Real-time temperature monitoring. Adjusts automatically to stay within safe limits. No thermal shutdown, no degradation over time.
  • Garbage Collection + TRIM — Runs in the background, clearing space you didn't know you needed. Windows TRIM supported natively. Writes stay fast years from now.
  • Early Move — Detects aging cells before they fail. Moves data proactively. Predictive protection, not reactive.
  • Wear Leveling — Writes distributed evenly across all cells. Every cell works equally. Every cell lasts equally. The drive ages gracefully.
  • ECC Error Correction — Automatic error detection and correction on every operation. Data integrity maintained continuously — without performance cost.

The best tool is the one you forget exists.
