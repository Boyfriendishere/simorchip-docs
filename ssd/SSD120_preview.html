04 — Safer
Safer
4 layers of protection. Not a single bit lost.
Every photo you've ever taken.
Every document you can't recreate.
Every file that matters.
  • Power Loss Protection (PLP) — Sudden power cut? PLP commits all in-flight data before shutdown. No corruption. No lost files.
  • ECC Error Correction — Hardware-level error detection and correction on every read. Automatic. Silent. Always on.
  • Bad Block Management — Failing cells isolated and bypassed automatically. Routed around before reaching your data.
  • Wear Leveling — Writes distributed evenly across all cells. Nothing wears out first. The drive ages uniformly.

You don't have to think about data protection.
