Strongest

Built to outlast every task it's given.

1,500,000 hours. 3,000 TBW on the 4TB model.

The fastest drive in its class. Also the most enduring.

  • 8-Channel Controller — Parallel Throughput Architecture — Eight simultaneous data channels reading and writing at once. This is what 7,200 MB/s actually requires — and the SSD 430 delivers it sustained, not just at burst.

視覺：8 通道並行架構示意圖 — 8 條資料流同時進出，對比 4 通道競品

強調：持續吞吐量，不只是峰值

  • DRAM Cache — On-board Dedicated Buffer — A dedicated DRAM chip stores the full address mapping table. Every data request finds its target instantly — no guessing, no extra latency. DRAM-less SSDs slow down under sustained workloads. The SSD 430 doesn't.

視覺：DRAM Cache 示意圖 — 有無 DRAM 的延遲對比曲線

有 DRAM：延遲線平直 / 無 DRAM：延遲線隨工作量升高波動

  • High-quality 3D NAND Flash + SLC Caching — SLC cache delivers outstanding burst read/write performance. 3D NAND stacking provides higher density and better endurance under sustained load.

視覺：3D NAND 垂直堆疊截面示意圖 + SLC cache 工作原理

  • Up to 4TB capacity — 512GB / 1TB / 2TB / 4TB. The 1TB is the performance sweet spot. The 4TB holds your entire creative pipeline — a decade of work, on one drive.

03-Smarter

Smarter

The fastest drive is also the most intelligent

DRAM Cache. 8-Channel Controller. SLC Caching.
The intelligence that makes 7,200 MB/s not just possible — but sustainable.

It’s more consistent than your daily Starbucks order. No bitter surprises, just pure speed.

DRAM 快取、8 通道控制器、SLC 快取技術
這份智慧讓 7,200 MB/s 不只是可能，而是常態

比你每天星巴克喝到的那杯咖啡還穩——不苦澀，只有順

  • LDPC Error Correction (Low-Density Parity Check) — Hardware-level error detection and correction on every read and write. Data integrity maintained automatically, without any performance cost.

視覺：LDPC 錯誤偵測修正流程圖 — 錯誤位元紅色標示 → 修正後綠色

  • Dynamic Thermal Throttling — A graphene composite layer applied at substrate level conducts heat away from NAND cells faster than conventional materials — this is why SSD 430 stays stable at 54°C under sustained load. Not by slowing down. By staying cool.
    視覺：熱成像對比 — SSD 430 均勻低溫（54°C）vs 競品熱點集中（78°C+）
  • NVMe Sets task distribution + PLM Predictable Latency Mode — Workloads distributed intelligently across NAND cells and channels. Lower latency, more consistent response under the heaviest mixed read/write loads.

視覺：8 通道任務均衡分配示意圖

  • Garbage Collection + TRIM — Runs in the background, clearing space you didn't know you needed. Writes stay fast years from now.

視覺：TRIM + GC 工作流程圖

  • Early Move — Detects aging cells before they fail. Moves data proactively. Predictive data protection, not reactive.

視覺：Early Move 預測性資料搬移流程示意

  • Wear Leveling — Writes spread evenly across all cells across all 8 channels. Every cell works equally. Every cell lasts equally.

視覺：磨損均衡分佈示意，各區塊顏色深淺代表使用程度，8 通道版本更均勻
