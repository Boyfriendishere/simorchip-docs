Validation

Testing & Validation Standards

Built, Tested, and Verified.

Beyond manufacturing flow, Simorchip products go through dedicated validation procedures to confirm capacity, performance, compatibility, stability, and product information before delivery.

On the Line

Automated Functional Testing

Memory modules run through automated test fixtures that check capacity, timing, and electrical parameters — every unit, every batch, before moving to the next stage. Removing manual variance keeps results consistent and repeatable.

Control Points

Inspection at Every Stage

Inspection doesn't wait until the end. It runs through every stage — to catch, correct, and confirm before anything ships.

On the Floor

Verified by Trained Inspectors

Dedicated QC stations confirm product information, appearance, and final test results for every batch — the last check before goods are approved for packing and shipment.
